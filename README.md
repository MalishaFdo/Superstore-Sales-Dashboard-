# Superstore-Sales-Dashboard-
Superstore Sales Dashboard also helps automate sales forecasting and inventory management through the availability of real-time data on geographic sales, sales performance, and units of products sold. It is a valuable tool that helps in decision-making and working procedure automation. It facilitates easy monitoring and tracking of matters like profitability, product demand, and sales trends, and it is simple to project future requirements and mobilize resources. The stakeholders are sales managers, inventory managers, and executives who can utilize this data to make informed decisions, maximize sales efforts, and raise regional stock levels. Through the presentation of key business insights graphically, the dashboard enables swift, practical action in dynamic retail environments.
